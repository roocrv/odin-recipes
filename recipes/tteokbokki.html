Tteokbokki

Image by jcomp on Freepik

Description

Tteokbokki is a staple of Korean street food. Fiery but extremely delicious, it's one of the easier Korean dishes to do at home if you're craving for something sweet and spicy dish.

Ingredients

  • Chili paste (gochujang)
  • Sugar
  • Soy sauce
  • Corn syrup
  • Rice cakes (tteok)
  • Fish cakes
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Fill a saucepan with water to bring to a boil.
  2. Combine gochujang, sugar, soy sauce and corn syrup in a bowl.
  3. Add your mixture to the pan, along with your rice cakes and fish cakes.
  4. Let it boil for six minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Once the rice cakes start getting softer, and the sauce thicker, it's ready to serve.
  6. Enjoy! 잘 먹겠습니다!
