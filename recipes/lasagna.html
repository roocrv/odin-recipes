Lasagna

Image by wirestock on Freepik

Description

Lasagna is a classical italian dish composed of multiple layers of meat, freshly cut pasta (aka lasagna noodles) and sauce. Some recipes may also include bechamel sauce and cheese

Ingredients

  • Ground beef OR Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasoning
  • Your favorite cheeses
  • Egg (Yes, one)

Steps

  1. Cook your ground beef, together with the onion and garlic, over medium heat until browned.
  2. Stir in your tomatoes (crushed, sauce, paste) and water. Season with the sugar, herbs, salt and pepper. Simmer, covered, for ninety minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Cook your lasagna noodles in boiling water for eight minutes more or less. Drain them, and rinse with cold water to stop them from overcooking.
  4. Prepare your cheeses, mixing them with some herbs. Add salt.
  5. Preheat your oven to 190 degrees celsius.
  6. To assemble the lasagna, spread evenly overlaying layers of meat, noodles, and cheese until you're out of ingredients.
  7. Bake the lasagna in the oven for twenty-five minutes.
  8. Let the lasagna rest for five minutes before serving.
  9. Enjoy!
